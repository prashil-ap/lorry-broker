About LorryBroker

Building the Future of Logistics Technology

We're on a mission to empower carriers with smarter tools, stronger lanes, and the technology they need to scale their businesses profitably.

Our Story

LorryBroker was founded in 2020 by a team of logistics veterans and technology innovators who saw a broken system. Owner-operators and small carriers were struggling with outdated load boards, inconsistent freight quality, and cash flow problems that threatened their businesses every single day.

We knew there had to be a better way. So we built it.

Today, LorryBroker serves thousands of carriers across 28 states, matching them with high-quality freight, reducing empty miles, and delivering the financial flexibility they need to grow. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI, real-time data, and human expertise to create a load-matching experience that actually works for professional carriers.

Our Mission

We exist to solve the biggest challenges facing modern carriers: inconsistent freight access, poor cash flow, and operational complexity that slows growth.

Our mission is simple: Keep trucks full. Keep revenue moving. Keep carriers in control.

Every feature we build, every partnership we forge, and every decision we make is guided by one principle: empowering carriers to build sustainable, profitable businesses on their own terms.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at LorryBroker:

Carrier-First

We build for carriers, not shippers. Every product decision starts with the question: "How does this help our carriers succeed?"

Transparency

No hidden fees. No surprise terms. No games. We believe trust is earned through radical honesty in every interaction.

Speed & Reliability

In logistics, every minute matters. We obsess over performance, uptime, and response times so carriers can move faster.

Innovation

The old way of doing things doesn't work anymore. We leverage technology to create solutions the industry has never seen.
